# Pricing

GistMag uses a simple, credit-based pricing system. Pay only for what you use, and credits never expire.

## Credit Costs

### Alt Text Generation
- **1 credit = 1 image alt text generation**
- Works for all modes: Short, Long, and SEO-optimized
- Batch processing uses the same rate (1 credit per image)

### Text-to-Speech (TTS)
- **1 credit = 1,000 characters of text**
- Minimum charge: 1 credit (even for text under 1,000 characters)
- Includes all TTS features: voice selection, speed, pitch, volume controls

### Speech-to-Text (STT)
- **5 credits = 1 minute of audio**
- Charges are rounded up to the nearest minute
- Supports multiple audio formats (MP3, WAV, etc.)

### Background Music
- **+1 credit** when adding background music to TTS
- This is in addition to the TTS credit cost
- Applies to both basic TTS with music and batch TTS with music

You can also purchase credits without a subscription. Credits never expire and can be used anytime.

## Credit Rollover

- Credits from subscriptions rollover indefinitely
- Unused credits accumulate in your account
- No expiration date - use them whenever you need
- New subscription credits are added to your existing balance
