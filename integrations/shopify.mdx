## Overview

The GistMag Shopify app automatically generates AI-powered alt text for product images in your Shopify store, improving accessibility and SEO.

## Features

- **Automatic Generation**: Generate alt text when products are created or updated
- **Bulk Processing**: Process all product images at once
- **Product Image Support**: Works with all product images
- **SEO Optimization**: SEO-optimized alt text for better search rankings
- **Accessibility**: Improves store accessibility for screen readers

## Installation

### From Shopify App Store

1. Log in to your Shopify admin
2. Go to **Apps** â†’ **App and sales channel settings**
3. Click **Shopify App Store**
4. Search for "GistMag"
5. Click **Add app**
6. Click **Install app**

### Configuration

1. After installation, go to the GistMag app in your Shopify admin
2. Connect your GistMag account or create a new one
3. Configure settings:
   - **Auto-generate**: Enable automatic generation for new products
   - **Default Mode**: Choose Short, Long, or SEO mode
   - **Language**: Select default language
4. Click **Save**

## Usage

### Automatic Generation

When enabled, alt text is automatically generated when:
- New products are created
- Product images are added
- Products are updated

### Manual Generation

1. Go to **Products** in your Shopify admin
2. Click on a product
3. Scroll to the **Images** section
4. Click **Generate Alt Text** on any image
5. Choose your mode and click **Generate**

### Bulk Processing

1. Go to the GistMag app in your Shopify admin
2. Click **Bulk Process**
3. Select products or all products
4. Choose mode and language
5. Click **Start Processing**

## Mode Guide

### Short Mode
- **Best for**: Product thumbnails, gallery images
- **Length**: 50-100 characters
- **Example**: "Blue cotton t-shirt"

### Long Mode
- **Best for**: Main product images, detailed shots
- **Length**: 100-200 characters
- **Example**: "Blue cotton t-shirt with round neck and short sleeves, displayed on a white background"

### SEO Mode
- **Best for**: All product images (recommended)
- **Features**: Includes product keywords and attributes
- **Example**: "Blue cotton t-shirt - men's casual wear - summer clothing"

<Info>
  SEO mode is recommended for Shopify stores as it helps improve search engine rankings while maintaining accessibility.
</Info>
